$F = 1 \times 10^{-17} W m^{-2}$

$\delta = 15 s$

$\sigma = \frac{4F}{\pi \delta^{2}}$

$d_{p} = 25 \mu m$

$s_{p} = \frac{206265 * d_{p}}{f}$

Albert:¶

$D_{A} = .3 m$

$f_{A} = 2.4 m$

$F_{A} = \sigma \frac{\pi D^{2}}{4}$

$E = 100 s_{p}^{2}F_{A} = 100 (\frac{206265 d_{p}}{f_{A}})^{2} \frac{4F}{\pi\delta^{2}}\frac{\pi D_{A}^2}{4}$
